General Information.

This project is about a simple Student Information System designed to systemize a Non for Profit Pre-K to 12 Private School. Is specifically tackle must of the daily administrative operations.

Features Includes

  • Registration Management
    • Parents information
    • Student information
    • Academic term information
    • Academic levels information
    • Student registration by academic level
  • Cashier
    • Invoice based on parent account
      • Recurrent fees payment
      • Nonrecurrent fees payments
    • Payments Methods: cash, check, credit
    • Other payments resgistration
